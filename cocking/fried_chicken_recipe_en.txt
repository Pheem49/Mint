Five-Star Style Fried Chicken Recipe (Crispy Outside, Juicy Inside, Aromatic Spices)

Ingredients for Marinating:
1. Chicken thighs or drumsticks - 1 kg
2. Coriander roots - 3-4 roots
3. Thai garlic - 10-15 cloves
4. White peppercorns - 1 tsp
5. Oyster sauce - 2 tbsp
6. Light soy sauce - 1 tbsp
7. Fish sauce - 1 tbsp
8. Sugar - 1 tsp
9. Chicken seasoning powder - 1 tsp (optional)

Batter Ingredients:
1. Crispy frying flour - 1 cup
2. Rice flour - 1/2 cup (for long-lasting crispiness)
3. Ice-cold water or soda water - 1 cup
4. A pinch of salt

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Marinade: Pound the coriander roots, garlic, and peppercorns (The Three Kings of Thai spices) together until fine.
2. Marinate the Chicken: Wash the chicken thoroughly and pat dry. Mix the chicken with the pounded spices, then add oyster sauce, light soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and seasoning powder. Massage well and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to 1 hour.
3. Prepare the Batter: Mix the crispy frying flour, rice flour, and salt together. Gradually pour in ice-cold water or soda water and stir until smooth (the consistency should be just right, not too thin).
4. First Fry: Dip the marinated chicken into the batter until well-coated. Fry in medium-heat oil until cooked through and the batter turns a light yellow color. Remove and let it rest for about 5 minutes.
5. Second Fry (Secret for Extra Crispiness): Increase the heat until the oil is very hot. Fry the chicken again briefly until the batter turns golden brown and very crispy.
6. Serve: Remove from oil, drain, and serve with sweet chili sauce or tomato sauce as preferred.
